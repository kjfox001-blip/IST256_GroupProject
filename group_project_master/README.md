We chose historical arms and Armour due to the shear variety and the ease in which to get pictures that follow certain aesthetics.

## How the website works
 This exanple works by printing out cards from our items.json on the shop or collections page and then allows the user to add them to a cart in whichever quantity they chose. the cart will calculate total price (subtotal) and then at checkoout information will be asked to complete the order along with its own orderID which is based on the date and time of the order. They may view this in the console which prints out all information including what was ordered, by whom, and where to deliver.
